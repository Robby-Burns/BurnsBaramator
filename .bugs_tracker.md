### BUGS-R07: Vercel Build Conflict (uv vs pip)
**Status:** Resolved
**Priority:** High
**Severity:** Blocker
**Found:** Feb 2026
**Fixed:** Feb 2026

**Problem:**
Vercel detected `pyproject.toml` and tried to use `uv`, but `vercel.json` forced a `pip install` command, leading to `externally-managed-environment` errors.

**Solution:**
Updated `.vercelignore` to exclude `pyproject.toml` and `uv.lock`. This forces Vercel to ignore the `uv` configuration and fall back to standard `pip` using `requirements-vercel.txt`.
